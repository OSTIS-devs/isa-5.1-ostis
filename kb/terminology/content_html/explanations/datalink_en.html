Datalink is a wire, cable, or electromagnetic network or bus system that connects field-located devices with dedicated microprocessors so that they share a common database and communicate according to a rigid protocol in a hierarchical and/or peer-to-peer relationship to other such devices and/or compatible microprocessor-based systems; see also communication link.
a) wire or cable networks may be twisted pair, coaxial, telephone, or fiber optic.
b) electromagnetic networks may be radio or microwave.
