High Level Control System (HLCS) is an a system that provides sophistication above that of the BPCS; with functions typically based in process computer or higher level hardware that interacts with the process by manipulating setpoints in the BPCS.
(a) Control functions in the HLCS include, but are not limited to, statistical process control and model predictive control.
(b) An HLCS is not necessary to operate a plant or process.
